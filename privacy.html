Privacy Policy for Desy (CV Builder App)

Last Updated: August 6, 2025

Thank you for using Desy. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information within the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, job title, email (if you sign in), and any details you provide in your CVs.
  • Images: Optional profile photo uploaded by the user to personalize their CV.
  • Authentication Data: Your email and login credentials via Firebase Authentication (if signed in).
  • Usage Data: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve performance and experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and customize CVs and PDF exports.
  • To store your CVs securely (in cloud or on device, depending on user status).
  • To offer premium features such as additional downloads, templates, and sync across devices.
  • To respond to support requests and improve the app.

We do not sell your personal information or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Logged-in users’ CVs are stored securely in Firebase Firestore.
  • Guest users’ data is stored only locally on the device.
  • We use secure connections (HTTPS) and encryption where possible.

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, and Storage)
  • Google Play Billing (to process premium purchases securely)

5. Access to Photos and Media

The app may request permission to access your photos solely to allow you to upload a profile image for your CV. This is entirely optional, and we do not access or scan your media without explicit action from you.

6. Your Rights

  • You may access, modify, or delete your information at any time from within the app.
  • You may request deletion of your account by emailing us at the address below.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may delete your account and associated data at any time. CVs are stored until you choose to delete them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app or on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
